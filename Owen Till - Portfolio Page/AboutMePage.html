About Me

Hello! I'm Owen Till

I’m a passionate game developer who has fun making any gameplay mechanic. I am a graduate from New England Institute Of Technology with a degree in Game Development And Simulation Programming. In my time at college, I have made many prototypes and finished low level games using mostly Unity with a little bit of Godot and Unreal.

My interests include gameplay programming, game testing, and just casually playing games. When I’m not coding, I am usually playing games for fun and inspiration, or watching movies.

My goal is to continue making fun game mechanics and blending them into full games. I would also like to collaborate with more creative minds in the industry.
